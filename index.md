#Privacy Policy for Tessera

Last updated: May 8, 2026

## Overview

Tessera is a free board game price tracker for the Serbian market. This privacy policy explains how your data is handled when you use Tessera.
Data Collection
Tessera collects the following personal data when you create an account:

Email address - used for authentication and account management
Username - a display name you choose when registering
FCM token - a device identifier used to deliver push notifications

If you sign in with Google, we receive your email address and display name from Google. No additional Google account data is accessed or stored.

## Data Storage

Your account data is stored securely in Google Firebase, a cloud service provided by Google LLC. This includes your email, username, notification preferences, and watchlist. Data is stored on Firebase servers and is subject to Google's privacy policy.

## Data Usage

The data we collect is used exclusively to:

Authenticate you and maintain your account
Display your watchlist of tracked board games
Send push notifications about price changes and stock availability for games you are watching, based on your notification preferences

We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising or analytics purposes.

## Notifications

Tessera uses Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to deliver push notifications. Notifications are only sent if you have enabled them in the app settings and for the specific game events you have opted into (price drops, price increases, back in stock, out of stock). You can disable notifications at any time in the app settings or through your device notification settings.

## Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from the Profile screen within the app. Deleting your account permanently removes your email, username, watchlist, and notification preferences from our servers. This action cannot be undone.

## Third Party Services

Tessera uses the following third party services:

Google Firebase - authentication, cloud database, and push notifications. Subject to Google's privacy policy.
Google Sign-In - optional authentication method. Subject to Google's privacy policy.
Google Play Billing - used for optional in-app tips. The developer receives no personal information from these transactions beyond the payment itself.

## Internet Connection

Tessera requires an active internet connection to function. The app communicates with Firebase servers to load board game data, sync your watchlist, and deliver notifications.

## Children's Privacy

Tessera is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

## Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes in the future, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new date.
